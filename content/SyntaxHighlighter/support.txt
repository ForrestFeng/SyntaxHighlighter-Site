## Support

SyntaxHighlighter 2.0 users receive priority support on the [support forums](http://alexgorbatchev.com/forums). If you are experiencing technical difficulties integrating SyntaxHighlighter, have CSS issues, other questions or concerns, please search in the [support forums](http://alexgorbatchev.com/forums) and if you don't find an answer, leave a message and it will be answered as soon as possible.
